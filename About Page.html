About Us


Daisy, Charlotte and Annabel all met on the beginners course of CodeFirst:Girls and bonded over a love of coffee (despite Charlotte being allergic to caffine)!

Together, we decided to build a website which would instruct people on the many different types of coffee and how to make them. There are copious amounts of coffee so the website is continually expanding!
